Holz100 — Brand Evolution Holz100 — Brand Evolution

Translating complex product benefits into clear, scalable content across web and marketing channels

  • Role: Graphic Designer / Content Designer
  • Tools: Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Mailchimp, Hootsuite
  • Timeline: Multiphase

Context

Holz100 is a prefab construction system centered around solid wood building technology, offering a range of environmental, health, and performance benefits. The challenge was to communicate these technical advantages in a way that is clear, engaging, and accessible to a broader audience—including homeowners, developers, and industry professionals.

Problem

The existing content relied heavily on technical language and lacked visual structure, making it difficult for users to quickly understand the value of the system.

Approach

To improve comprehension, I designed a custom icon system representing the 14 key benefits of the Holz100 building system. This system allowed users to quickly grasp key benefits without reading dense text.

  • Simplified complex concepts into clear visual symbols
  • Created a consistent style for use across web and marketing
  • Improved scannability and user understanding
