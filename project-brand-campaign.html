Strides and Smiles — Brand & Campaign Strides and Smiles — Brand & Campaign

Building a friendly, community-driven brand and campaign to launch a local dog walking service

  • Role: Brand Designer / Graphic Designer
  • Tools: Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, AI-assisted tools (ChatGPT), AI image generation tools
  • Timeline: ~1-2 weeks

Context

Strides and Smiles is a dog walking service designed to serve the local Strathroy community with a warm, reliable, and approachable brand presence. The goal was to create a cohesive identity and campaign that would resonate with dog owners, build trust quickly, and support early customer acquisition.

Problem

New local service businesses often struggle to stand out and build credibility, especially when competing with larger platforms or informal providers.

Approach

I developed a brand and campaign system centered around warmth, personality, and simplicity—making the service feel both professional and friendly - focused on capturing the joy of everyday dog walks while reinforcing reliability and care.

Brand Identity

The visual identity was designed to feel light, playful, and trustworthy.

  • Name & Tagline: Strides and Smiles — “Strut. Sniff. Smile.”
  • Typography: Clean, modern type paired with approachable styling
  • Color Palette: Warm, inviting tones to evoke friendliness and care
  • Visual Language: Soft shapes and playful elements inspired by movement and dogs

Outcome

The final result is a cohesive brand and campaign system that positions Strides and Smiles as a friendly, trustworthy, and professional local service. By keeping the brand approachable while maintaining structure and consistency, the design supports both emotional connection and practical decision-making.

Next Steps

With further development, the brand could expand through:

  • A dedicated website or booking platform
  • Local partnerships and community activations
  • Expanded campaign assets (flyers, signage, referrals)
  • Performance tracking and optimization of ad creatives
