Pulse — SaaS Social Media
Analytics Platform Pulse — SaaS Social Media
Analytics Platform

I designed a multi-channel campaign and product interface to help users turn social data into actionable insights.

  • Role: Graphic Designer / Product Designer
  • Tools: Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, AI-assisted tools
  • Timeline: ~1-2 weeks

Context

Pulse is a conceptual SaaS product designed to help marketers and small businesses track social media performance, understand engagement trends, and make data-driven decisions.

This project explores how a cohesive design system can be applied across product interfaces and marketing touchpoints, including dashboard UI, landing pages, and social media campaigns.

Problem

Many social media tools present data in ways that feel overwhelming or difficult to act on. Users often struggle to:

  • Quickly understand performance trends
  • Identify which content is working
  • Translate data into clear next steps

At the same time, marketing touchpoints (ads, landing pages, emails) are often disconnected from the product experience, leading to inconsistent messaging and user expectations.

Approach

The approach focused on simplifying complex data into clear, digestible insights while maintaining a cohesive visual system across both product and marketing touchpoints. Emphasis was placed on clarity, consistency, and usability to ensure a seamless user experience.

Visual System

I developed a clean, modern SaaS design system using:

  • A minimal color palette with a strong primary accent
  • Consistent typography and spacing
  • Reusable components (cards, buttons, charts)

Product Design

I designed a structured dashboard and analytics experience to support:

  • At-a-glance performance metrics (KPI cards)
  • Trend visualization through charts
  • Deeper exploration via filters and data tables

Marketing Campaign

I created a multi-channel campaign to promote the product and drive signups:

  • Instagram feed and story ads
  • LinkedIn ad for a professional audience
  • Landing page aligned with product experience

Outcome

This project demonstrates the ability to design across multiple touchpoints, from product UI to marketing assets, while maintaining a cohesive brand system. By focusing on clarity and consistency, the design simplifies complex data and aligns user expectations from first interaction (ads) through to product experience.
